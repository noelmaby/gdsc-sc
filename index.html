Hackathon is planned as a Hybrid model.The program will be conducted online and Project presentation will be conducted offline.

What should you be working on

Build a solution to a local problem using Google technologies, in accordance with one or mnore of the United Nations 17 sustainable Development Goals.

No Poverty

End poverty in all its forms, everywhere. This goal aims to eradicate extreme poverty and reduce overall poverty levels worldwide by 2023.

Zero Hunger

This goal aims to end hunger, achieve food security, improved nutrition among different nations, and promote sustainable agriculture by 2030

Good Health and Wellbeing

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all, at all ages. It recognizes the importance of access to quality healthcare, safe and nutritions food, clean water, and sanitation.

Quality Education

It ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all, recognizes the transformative power of education to break the cycle of poverty.

Gender Equality

Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls. It recognizes that gender inequality is a root cause of poverty and social injustice and that empowering women and girls is essential for sustainable development.

Clean Water and Sanitation

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all human beings, which is a basic human right and essential for human health and environmental sustainability.
